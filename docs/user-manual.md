# Rasta Collections — Admin User Manual

This guide explains how to use the admin panel to manage your Rasta Collections website.

---

## Accessing the Admin Panel

1. Go to **your-domain.com/admin/login** in your browser
2. Enter your username and password
3. Click **Login**

You'll be taken to the Dashboard.

---

## Dashboard

The Dashboard shows you a quick overview of your store:

- **Total Products** — how many products you've added
- **Visible** — how many are currently visible to customers
- **Featured** — how many are marked as featured (shown on the homepage)
- **Unread Messages** — new contact form messages you haven't read yet

Use the quick action buttons to jump to common tasks.

---

## Managing Products

### Adding a Product

1. Click **Products** in the sidebar
2. Click the **Add Product** button
3. Fill in the form:
   - **Title** — the product name (required)
   - **Description** — a short description of the product
   - **Price** — the selling price (required)
   - **Category** — choose from your categories (required)
   - **eBay URL** — paste the eBay listing link so customers can buy it
   - **Visible** — toggle ON to show the product on the website
   - **Featured** — toggle ON to feature it on the homepage (max 4 recommended)
4. Click **Create Product**

### Adding Images

After creating a product, you can add images:

1. Open the product by clicking **Edit**
2. Scroll down to the **Images** section
3. Click the upload area or drag and drop image files
4. The first image will be used as the main product image
5. Drag images to reorder them
6. Click the **X** button on any image to remove it

Images are automatically compressed and converted to WebP format for fast loading.

### Editing a Product

1. Go to **Products** in the sidebar
2. Click the **Edit** button (pencil icon) on the product you want to change
3. Make your changes
4. Click **Save Changes**

### Showing/Hiding Products

You can quickly show or hide products without editing them:

- In the Products list, use the **Visible** toggle switch
- Hidden products won't appear on the public website

### Featuring Products

Featured products appear on the homepage:

- In the Products list, use the **Featured** toggle switch
- Up to 4 featured products are shown on the homepage
- If no products are marked as featured, the 4 newest visible products are shown instead

### Deleting a Product

1. In the Products list, click the **Delete** button (bin icon)
2. Confirm the deletion in the popup

**Warning:** Deleting a product is permanent and also removes all its images.

---

## Managing Categories

Categories help organise your products.

### Adding a Category

1. Click **Categories** in the sidebar
2. Type the category name in the text field
3. Click **Add**

A URL-friendly slug is automatically generated (e.g. "Coins & Tokens" becomes "coins-tokens").

### Editing a Category

1. Click the **Edit** button (pencil icon) next to the category
2. Change the name
3. Click **Save**

### Deleting a Category

1. Click the **Delete** button (bin icon) next to the category
2. Confirm the deletion

**Note:** You cannot delete a category that still has products in it. Move or delete those products first.

---

## Viewing Contact Messages

When a customer fills out the contact form on the website, their message appears here.

1. Click **Messages** in the sidebar
2. Unread messages are highlighted in bold
3. Click **View** to read a message — it will be marked as read automatically
4. Click **Reply via Email** to open your email app with the customer's address pre-filled
5. Click **Delete** to remove a message

---

## Tips

- **Homepage layout:** The homepage shows your featured products. Keep 3-4 products featured for the best look.
- **Product images:** Use clear, well-lit photos. Square images (same width and height) look best.
- **eBay links:** Always add the eBay URL so customers can buy directly.
- **Categories:** Keep category names short and clear. They appear in the website navigation bar.
- **Check messages regularly:** Customers expect a reply within a day or two.

---

## Logging Out

Click the **Logout** button at the bottom of the sidebar to sign out.
